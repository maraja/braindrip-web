# Step 7: Launch and Maintain

One-Line Summary: Connect a custom domain, run through a pre-launch checklist, publish your portfolio, and set up a quarterly refresh workflow.

Prerequisites: Steps 1–6 complete, portfolio site designed and SEO-optimized

---

## Pre-Launch Checklist

Go through every item before you hit publish:

### Content Check

- Hero section — clear headline, subheading, and CTA
- About page — bio tells your story, not just your resume
- Projects — at least 3 case studies with results
- Contact — working form or email link
- All copy proofread (paste into AI for a final grammar check)

### Technical Check

- All links work (internal and external)
- Images load on mobile and desktop
- Page loads in under 3 seconds
- Forms submit correctly (test with your own email)
- Favicon uploaded
- Social sharing preview (Open Graph image and description) set

### SEO Check

- Page titles set for every page
- Meta descriptions written
- Alt text on all images
- Google Analytics connected
- Sitemap generated and accessible

### Proofreading Prompt

```
Proofread this website copy for:
1. Grammar and spelling errors
2. Inconsistent tone or voice
3. Vague claims that could be more specific
4. Missing calls to action
5. Sentences that are too long or unclear

Here is the copy:

[PASTE ALL YOUR WEBSITE COPY]
```

## Connect Your Domain

### Option 1: Custom Domain (Recommended)

1. Buy a domain from Namecheap, Google Domains, or Cloudflare ($10–15/year)
2. In your site builder (Framer/Webflow/Carrd), go to Settings then Custom Domain
3. Add your domain and follow the DNS instructions
4. Wait for DNS propagation (usually 15 minutes to 24 hours)
5. Enable HTTPS (most platforms do this automatically)

**Naming tips**:
- `yourname.com` is the gold standard
- `yourname.dev` or `yourname.design` for role-specific domains
- Keep it short, memorable, and easy to spell

### Option 2: Free Subdomain

All platforms offer free subdomains like `yourname.framer.website`. This is fine for starting out but looks less professional.

## Launch Day

### Share Your Portfolio

Use AI to draft announcements for each platform:

```
I just launched my personal portfolio at [URL].
I am a [ROLE] who specializes in [SKILLS].

Write 3 launch announcements:
1. A LinkedIn post (professional, 150-200 words)
2. A Twitter/X post (casual, under 280 characters)
3. An email to my network (warm, personal, 100 words)

Each should include a clear call to action to visit the site.
```

### Places to Share

- **LinkedIn** — Post and update your profile with the URL
- **Twitter/X** — Pin the announcement tweet
- **Email** — Send to your professional contacts
- **GitHub** — Add the URL to your profile README
- **Resume** — Replace the old link everywhere
- **Email signature** — Add your portfolio link

## Set Up Quarterly Maintenance

Your portfolio should evolve with your career. Set a calendar reminder every 3 months:

### Quarterly Review Prompt

```
Here is my current portfolio copy:

[PASTE CURRENT COPY]

Since the last update, I have:
- [NEW PROJECT OR ACHIEVEMENT]
- [NEW SKILL OR CERTIFICATION]
- [NEW ROLE OR CLIENT]

Update my portfolio copy to:
1. Add the new project as a case study (use the same format as existing ones)
2. Update my bio to reflect new experience
3. Refresh the skills section
4. Suggest any sections I should add or remove
5. Update the SEO meta description
```

### Maintenance Checklist

| Task | Frequency |
|------|-----------|
| Add new projects | As completed |
| Update bio and skills | Quarterly |
| Check all links work | Quarterly |
| Review analytics — top pages, bounce rate | Monthly |
| Refresh hero section copy | Every 6 months |
| Update headshot / visual branding | Annually |

## Use Analytics to Improve

After a month, review your Google Analytics:

```
Here are my portfolio analytics for the past month:

- Total visitors: [N]
- Top pages: [LIST]
- Average time on site: [N] seconds
- Bounce rate: [X]%
- Traffic sources: [BREAKDOWN]

Based on this data:
1. Which projects are getting the most attention?
2. Is my bounce rate healthy? If not, what should I change?
3. What content should I add to keep visitors engaged longer?
4. How can I drive more traffic to the site?
```
