# Step 1: What We're Building

One-Line Summary: A polished personal portfolio website with AI-written copy, SEO optimization, and project showcases — built entirely without code.

Prerequisites: A free account on Claude or ChatGPT, a free account on Framer, Webflow, or Carrd, basic computer skills

---

## The Goal

By the end of this blueprint, you will have:

- **A live portfolio website** with a custom domain and professional design
- **AI-written copy** that sounds like you, not a robot
- **Project case studies** that highlight your impact and results
- **SEO optimization** so people can actually find you on Google
- **A maintenance workflow** to keep your portfolio fresh with AI assistance

No coding. No design degree. No expensive tools. Just you, an AI assistant, and a drag-and-drop builder.

## Who This Is For

This blueprint is built for three groups:

| Audience | What You'll Get |
|----------|----------------|
| **Freelancers** | A client-facing portfolio that wins projects and justifies your rates |
| **Job seekers** | A personal site that stands out beyond a resume and LinkedIn profile |
| **Creators & makers** | A showcase for your work that lives on your own domain |

If you have work to show but no website to show it on, this is your blueprint.

## The Tools (All Free or Low-Cost)

| Tool | Purpose | Cost |
|------|---------|------|
| **Claude or ChatGPT** | Write all your copy, bios, case studies, and SEO content | Free tier works fine |
| **Framer** | Drag-and-drop website builder with beautiful templates | Free plan available |
| **Webflow** | More powerful builder with CMS capabilities | Free plan available |
| **Carrd** | Simplest option — single-page portfolio sites | Free plan, $19/year for pro |
| **Unsplash** | Free, high-quality stock photos | Free |
| **Google Analytics** | Track who visits your site | Free |

You only need one website builder. We will help you choose the right one in Step 5.

## What Makes This Different

Most portfolio guides tell you to "just write about yourself." That is the hardest part. This blueprint flips the process:

1. **You provide the raw material** — your skills, your projects, your rough notes
2. **AI transforms it** — into polished copy, compelling case studies, and SEO content
3. **You refine and publish** — because you know your voice better than anyone

The AI does the heavy lifting. You stay in the driver's seat.

## The End Result

Your finished portfolio will include:

- **Homepage** with a compelling hero section and clear value proposition
- **About page** with a professional bio that tells your story
- **Projects/work page** with 3-5 detailed case studies
- **Contact section** with a clear call to action
- **SEO foundations** with proper meta titles, descriptions, and image alt text

## What Each Step Covers

1. **This overview** — what we are building and why
2. **Define your brand** — nail down who you are, what you do, and who you serve
3. **Write your copy** — use AI to craft every section of your site
4. **Showcase your projects** — turn rough notes into engaging case studies
5. **Design and build** — pick a platform, choose a template, and assemble your site
6. **SEO and optimization** — make sure people can find you on Google
7. **Launch and maintain** — go live and set up a quarterly update workflow

Total time: 4-8 hours spread across a weekend. Let's start by defining your personal brand.
