# Step 7: Schedule and Publish

One-Line Summary: Load your AI-generated content into a scheduling tool and set it to publish automatically across all your platforms.

Prerequisites: Steps 1–6 complete, post copy and visuals ready for at least one week

---

## Choose Your Scheduling Tool

| Tool | Free Tier | Best For |
|------|-----------|----------|
| **Buffer** | 3 channels, 10 posts/channel | Solopreneurs, simple scheduling |
| **Later** | 1 social set, 30 posts/month | Visual-first brands (Instagram) |
| **Hootsuite** | 2 accounts, 5 scheduled posts | Teams, multiple platforms |
| **Meta Business Suite** | Unlimited (Facebook + Instagram only) | Meta-only brands |

Pick one and connect your social media accounts.

## Bulk Upload Your Content

Most scheduling tools support CSV or bulk upload. Here is how to prepare your content:

### Buffer CSV Format

```
Text, Link, Image URL, Publish Date, Publish Time, Platform
"Your post copy here", https://link.com, https://image-url.com, 2025-03-24, 09:00, twitter
```

### Prompt to Generate a CSV

```
I have these posts ready for the week:

[PASTE YOUR POST LIST]

Format them as a CSV with columns:
Text, Link (if any), Platform, Publish Date, Publish Time

Schedule them across these time slots:
- Twitter/X: 9 AM, 12 PM, 5 PM
- LinkedIn: 8 AM, 12 PM
- Instagram: 11 AM, 7 PM

Start date: [DATE]. Spread posts evenly across the week.
```

## Optimal Posting Times

These are general starting points — adjust based on your analytics:

| Platform | Best Days | Best Times |
|----------|-----------|------------|
| **Twitter/X** | Tue–Thu | 9 AM, 12 PM, 5 PM |
| **LinkedIn** | Tue–Thu | 7–8 AM, 12 PM, 5–6 PM |
| **Instagram** | Mon–Fri | 11 AM–1 PM, 7–9 PM |
| **Facebook** | Wed–Fri | 9 AM, 1 PM, 3 PM |

## Preview Before Publishing

Before you hit schedule, check every post:

- **Character limits**: Twitter (280), LinkedIn (3,000), Instagram caption (2,200)
- **Hashtags**: Present and relevant for each platform
- **Images**: Correct dimensions, text readable, brand colors consistent
- **Links**: Working and tracked (use UTM parameters if needed)
- **Mentions/tags**: Correct handles for each platform

### Preview Checklist Prompt

```
Review these scheduled posts for errors:

[PASTE POSTS]

Check for:
1. Posts exceeding character limits for their platform
2. Broken or missing hashtags
3. Tone inconsistencies with this brand voice: [YOUR BRAND VOICE]
4. Any post that could be misinterpreted or controversial
5. Duplicate or too-similar content

Flag any issues.
```

## Set Up a Recurring Workflow

Here is your weekly content publishing routine:

| Day | Task | Time |
|-----|------|------|
| **Monday** | Review last week's analytics (Step 8) | 15 min |
| **Monday** | Generate this week's post copy with AI | 30 min |
| **Tuesday** | Create visuals in Canva | 30 min |
| **Tuesday** | Upload and schedule all posts | 20 min |
| **Daily** | Check for comments, reply or engage | 10 min |

**Total: ~2.5 hours/week** for a full social media presence across multiple platforms.

## Tips for Consistency

1. **Batch create** — Do not post one at a time. Create a full week of content in one sitting.
2. **Use templates** — Save your best-performing post formats as reusable templates.
3. **Queue evergreen content** — Recycle your best posts every 60–90 days.
4. **Leave room for real-time** — Schedule 80% of posts, keep 20% open for timely or spontaneous content.
5. **Save drafts** — If AI generates a great post but the timing is wrong, save it for later.
